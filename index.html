About

Biography

I am a Civil Engineer currently living in Miami, Florida with a strong background in water resources, urban drainage modeling, and climate-resilient infrastructure planning. I hold a BSc in Civil and Water Resources Engineering from the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST), Bangladesh, and currently work remotely as a Senior Environmental Engineer at Streams Tech Ltd (USA), where I develop hydrodynamic and stormwater models for urban flood mitigation in both national and international projects.

My research interests lie at the intersection of hydrological modeling, GIS & remote sensing, and sustainable urban water management. I have presented my work at international conferences including the AGU Fall Meeting and co-authored several publications on integrated water resources management and climate adaptation strategies.

I am now seeking graduate research opportunities (MS/PhD) in Civil or Environmental Engineering in the USA to further explore data-driven solutions for water-related challenges in a changing climate.
